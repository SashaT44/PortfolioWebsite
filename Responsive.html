ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Sasha. I'm an aspiring graphic designer currently studying at NYC College of Technology. I've spent years developing my artistic skills across various mediums, specifically through drawing.

I've completed two design internships. In one, I created an Anti-Hate Initiative flyer for the event opening. In the other, I helped develop the initial visual identity and branding for a new project, including a style guide.

I'm passionate about creating work that pushes me to think outside the box and I'm always eager to learn new skills.
